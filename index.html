My Work:

Steel Railings

Fabricated and assembled custom steel railings from square tubing, performing all fit-up, tacking, and MIG welding on joints and pickets. Installed a temporary brace to maintain squareness and alignment during welding, preventing distortion and ensuring accuracy.

Sheet-Metal Countertop Display Rack

Fabricated and TIG welded a custom display rack from cold-rolled steel, designed for point-of-purchase use near cash registers to hold small packaged products.

Steel Bollard

Fabricated and welded a steel bollard. Attached and blended a rounded cap, then welded the base plate with flux-core for structural strength.

Safe-T-Cap Rust Repair Kits

Fabricated and welded rust-repair frame kits for automotive applications at Safe-T-Cap. Used short arc MIG welding on 11-gauge cold-rolled steel, producing clean, consistent welds and accurate fit-up to factory frame geometry. Followed production drawings closely to ensure proper alignment and a clean appearance for easy installation.

Services

Fit-Up & Layout
